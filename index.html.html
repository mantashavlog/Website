Integral University has central as well as departmental library. The central library has the collection of more than 0.1 million documents comprising of books, theses, journals, dissertations and CDs/DVDs in the fields of applied sciences, bio sciences, engineering, medical sciences, education, humanities, literature and management with a seating capacity of 600 readers. The library collection includes 99255 volumes.
Integral University has 3 hostels for boys and 2 hostels for girls. The facilities provided in the hostels are common room equipped with indoor game facilities, playground and courts for outdoor games like badminton, volleyball, cricket, skating, lawn tennis, basketball, etc., gymnasium, 24 Hrs. wi-fi, water purifier, parking, guest rooms, reading room.
